## What is it?

German sovereign cloud providers (Schwarz Gruppe / Deutsche Telekom) — full EU jurisdiction, free of US Cloud Act exposure.

## Why this ring?

Our sovereign answer for clients where hyperscalers are excluded (KRITIS, public sector, banking). Trial rather than Adopt: the service catalog is far smaller than AWS/Azure and our production experience is still limited — every engagement here needs a champion.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** when data sovereignty is a hard, contractual requirement.
- **Don't** position it as a hyperscaler replacement for general workloads — expect to self-manage much more of the stack.
