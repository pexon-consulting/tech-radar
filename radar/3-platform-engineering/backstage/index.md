## What is it?

Internal Developer Platform frameworks: Backstage (open source, build-it-yourself) and Port (SaaS). Typically combined with Crossplane for self-service infrastructure provisioning.

## Why this ring?

Our IDP recommendation in platform engineering engagements. Trial: IDP projects are heavyweight and success depends heavily on client maturity — each engagement needs a champion and an honest write-up here.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** when the client has 5+ product teams and a platform team to own the IDP.
- **Don't** sell an IDP to organizations without a platform team — it becomes shelfware.
