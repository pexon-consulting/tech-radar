## What is it?

The governance practice around every AI system we ship: EU-AI-Act risk classification, DSGVO/AVV, audit logging, SSO/RBAC enforcement, and sector rules (DORA, BAIT, NIS2) — including external governance boards as a service.

## Why this ring?

Non-negotiable on client projects — every Layer-1-to-6 delivery passes through this. The practice is Adopt even where individual tools beneath it are still being standardized (→ LLM Guardrails entry).

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** the risk-classification + audit-logging baseline on every AI engagement, before the first model call ships.
- **Don't** treat it as a paperwork phase at the end — governance requirements shape architecture (hosting, logging, HiTL) from day one.
