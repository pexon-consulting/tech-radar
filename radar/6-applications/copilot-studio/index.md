## What is it?

Microsoft's low-code stack for departmental AI apps and copilots — Copilot Studio for conversational agents, PowerApps for forms-and-workflow apps, with connectors into SAP, SharePoint and ServiceNow.

## Why this ring?

The fastest route to departmental AI in Microsoft-committed organizations; we used it for prototyping at Currenta and integrate it with SAP via MCP. Trial: great for prototypes and departmental tools, watch the licensing costs and customization ceiling.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** for departmental use cases in M365-heavy organizations where time-to-value beats flexibility.
- **Don't** build complex, latency-critical or highly custom agents on it (→ Layer 5 frameworks).
