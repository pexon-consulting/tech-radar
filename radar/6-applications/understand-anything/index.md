## What is it?

Converts codebases into graph-based representations for AI-assisted understanding. Useful when onboarding into large legacy codebases as part of a modernization or AI integration project.

## Why this ring?

A discovery/analysis-phase tool we are evaluating — explicitly not a production runtime dependency.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** during discovery phases of projects with large existing codebases.
- **Don't** ship it as part of a delivered solution.
