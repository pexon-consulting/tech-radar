## What is it?

Framework for role-based multi-agent "crews" — agents with roles, goals and shared tasks.

## Why this ring?

High mindshare and fast to demo; we are assessing whether the abstraction holds up in production or fights us once workflows need fine control (where LangGraph's explicit graphs win).

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** for rapid multi-agent prototypes and internal experiments.
- **Don't** commit client production workloads to it yet.
