## What is it?

Low-code workflow automation with native AI/agent nodes — self-hostable, source-available, German vendor (relevant for data-residency conversations).

## Why this ring?

The pragmatic middle ground for business-process automation: faster than custom code, more controllable than SaaS-only tools, and it runs inside the client's perimeter.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** for departmental AI workflows (HR, sales, service) where a full custom build is overkill.
- **Don't** use it as the backbone of complex, latency-critical agent systems (→ LangGraph).
