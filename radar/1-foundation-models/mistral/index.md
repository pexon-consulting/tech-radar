## What is it?

European model family — open-weight models for self-hosting (via vLLM/SGLang) plus the managed La Plateforme, available through Azure AI Foundry as well.

## Why this ring?

The strongest "European AI" answer in sovereignty conversations — EU jurisdiction matters to exactly the clients who exclude US hyperscaler models. Named in our public stack (Foundry) and open-source posts; Assess until a project commits and benchmarks it against Llama/DeepSeek for the use case.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** in evaluations where EU provenance of the model vendor is a selling point or requirement.
- **Don't** pick it on provenance alone — benchmark per use case like any other open-weight model.
