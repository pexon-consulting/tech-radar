## What is it?

Lightweight CLI search engine for local docs, knowledge bases, and meeting notes using embeddings.

## Why this ring?

Good for quickly adding semantic search to a project's internal documentation, or during a pilot before a full vector DB setup is warranted.

## When to use it — and when not?

- **Use** for prototyping retrieval on small corpora.
- **Don't** treat it as a production replacement for Qdrant/pgvector.
